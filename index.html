About Me

I'm a
self-taught baker without a passion for sugary stuff

I was born in Kunming, Yunnan, China and moved around a little bit after 18. I have ADD and it is really hard for me to have a long attention span, so my parents gave me a dog. Since then, I look at the world through dog's eyes. When I began to live in Seattle, my imaginary dog wasn't with me anymore, thus I got really bored and started to bake. But looking at all the fat ducks in the world, I really don't want to become one, so I decided to become a sugarless baker. Drop mic.
